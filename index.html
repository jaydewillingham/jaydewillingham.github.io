I am currently pursuing a Master of Research at Macquarie University, where I investigate the evolution of star formation rates over cosmic history. This research is an exciting step in my journey toward completing a PhD and advancing our understanding of galaxy evolution, the early universe, and fundamental cosmological questions.
Previously, I graduated from the University of Newcastle with a Bachelor of Science (majoring in Physics) and a Bachelor of Mathematics (majoring in Pure and Applied Mathematics). During my time there, I developed a passion for teaching and outreach, focusing on creating engaging, accessible content tailored to diverse abilities, learning levels, and styles.
To learn more about my experiences and qualifications, visit my CV page!
